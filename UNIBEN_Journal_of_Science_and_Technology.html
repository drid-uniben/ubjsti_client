About the Journal

The UNIBEN Journal of Science & Technology publishes peer-reviewed applied research in natural sciences, engineering, computing, climate/energy technologies, public-health technologies, and data & AI for development, with an emphasis on reproducibility and practical impact.

Editorial Policies

Open Access & Licensing

This journal is fully open access. Authors retain copyright and publish under the Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY 4.0) licence. No embargoes. Readers may read, share, and reuse with attribution. (Aligned with Plan S/open-access best practice.)

Peer Review

Double-anonymous review; at least two external reviewers per submission. Typical timelines: 3–6 weeks to first decision. Editors may request a third review in cases of disagreement. Exceptions (e.g., editorials) are clearly labelled.

Publication Ethics

We follow the COPE Core Practices, including handling of complaints/appeals, conflicts of interest, research misconduct (plagiarism, data fabrication), and corrections/retractions. Allegations are assessed using COPE flowcharts; outcomes are reported transparently on the journal site.

Data & Materials (where applicable)

All submissions must include a Data Availability Statement. Where ethical/legal limits apply, authors should state the conditions for access. For empirical work, authors are encouraged to share datasets/code in a trusted repository with a persistent identifier.

Copyright & Self-Archiving

Authors keep copyright. Posting of preprints and Author Accepted Manuscripts (AAMs) is permitted, provided the final published version is linked via its DOI.

Plagiarism & Similarity Screening

All submissions undergo similarity screening (e.g., Crossref Similarity Check/Turnitin). Manuscripts with significant, unexplained textual overlap may be rejected or retracted.

Article Processing Charges (APCs) / Fees

No APCs (Diamond OA). Supported by TETFund. Waiver policy therefore not required; any ancillary charges (e.g., colour pages in print) are disclosed in advance (if applicable).

Indexing & Persistent Identifiers

Articles are assigned Crossref DOIs and are structured for indexing by Google Scholar. The journal will apply to the Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ) once eligibility thresholds are met.

Archiving & Preservation

All content is preserved via the PKP Preservation Network (PN) and/or LOCKSS/CLOCKSS to ensure long-term accessibility.

Submissions

Please submit manuscripts via our online system (OJS link to be provided by UNIBEN IT/Library). Include ORCID iDs for all authors, ethics statements where required, and a Data Availability Statement for empirical work. Use a clear reference style and ensure plagiarism screening thresholds are respected.

Service Levels: 21 days to assign reviewers; 30–45 days to first decision; ≤14 days for author revisions; continuous online publication upon acceptance.
